1 Data Controller

The data controller for Mandala is an independent (indie) developer. For any privacy-related request, please contact us at the address listed in the Contact section below or via the game's Steam store page.

Mandala is built with Unity and uses GameAnalytics to collect anonymous gameplay data for the sole purpose of improving the game.

2 Data We Collect

Mandala collects only anonymous and pseudonymous data, aggregated analytics data through GameAnalytics. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2.1 — Device & Technical Data

  • Operating system and version
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Game version installed
  • Network connection type (not content)
  • Pseudonymous user identifier assigned by GameAnalytics (not linkable to a real identity)

2.2 — Session & Gameplay Data

  • Number of runs per session
  • Survival time per run
  • Distance traveled by the character
  • Number of pauses
  • Lowest FPS recorded (performance monitoring)
  • Best score achieved in the session
  • Total app session duration
  • Score snapshots over time (every 30 seconds)

2.3 — Combat & Progression Data

  • Enemy types and kill counts
  • Charged attacks used and cancelled
  • Projectiles fired, hits, and misses (accuracy rate)
  • Shield blocks
  • Enemy type that caused the player's death
  • Number of active enemies and spawn interval at time of death

2.4 — Upgrade & Powerup System Data

  • Powerups offered per type
  • Powerups selected per type
  • Number of upgrade rounds completed
  • Upgrade points earned
  • Average decision time in the selection screen
  • Maximum upgrade level reached
  • Final build composition at run end

2.5 — Behavioural Data

  • Pickups collected per type
  • Input method used (keyboard, controller, touch)
  • Quit context (pause menu, death screen, session end)
  • First-time player flag (anonymous boolean)

3 Purpose of Processing

All collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Improve game balance and design (death patterns, progression, combat mechanics)
  • Optimise technical performance across hardware configurations
  • Understand usage patterns to prioritise future updates and content
  • Detect and fix bugs or gameplay anomalies
Data is never used for advertising, commercial profiling, or sold to third parties.

4 Legal Basis

Processing of anonymous analytics data is based on the legitimate interest of the data controller (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) to improve the quality of the software product, balanced against users' fundamental rights and freedoms.

Where required by applicable law, explicit user consent may be requested prior to data collection.

5 GameAnalytics — Third-Party Service

Mandala uses GameAnalytics (GameAnalytics ApS, Copenhagen, Denmark) as its analytics platform. GameAnalytics acts as a data processor on behalf of the data controller.

  • GameAnalytics Privacy Policy: gameanalytics.com/privacy
  • GameAnalytics Terms of Service: gameanalytics.com/terms

Data is transmitted to GameAnalytics servers over an encrypted connection (HTTPS). GameAnalytics may store data on servers located in the European Union and/or the United States, subject to appropriate safeguards under EU data transfer regulations (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

6 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Payment or financial data (handled exclusively by Steam/Valve)
  • Precise geographic location
  • Chat content, messages, or communications
  • Steam account data (managed by Valve Corporation)
  • Identifiable data relating to minors

For information about your Steam account, purchase history, and personal data on the Steam platform, please refer to Valve's Privacy Policy.

7 Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained on GameAnalytics servers for the period necessary for statistical processing, generally no longer than 24 months. Because data is anonymous and cannot be linked to a specific individual, individual deletion rights under GDPR may not be technically applicable.

8 Your Rights (GDPR)

To the extent that data collected falls within the scope of the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request information about the data being processed
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request removal of your data
  • Restriction — request limitation of processing
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Portability — receive data in a structured, machine-readable format
Since all collected data is anonymous, it may not be technically possible to identify and isolate data relating to a specific user.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Italian data protection authority: Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali.

9 Children

Mandala is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect analytics data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided data, please contact us to request its removal.
